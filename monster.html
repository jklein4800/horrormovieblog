A Quiet Place Part II

I thought the first one was great but with part II it was disappointing. It failed to bring back the suspense of the first and was very cookie cutter of a film.

  • Director: John Krasinski
  • Release date: May 28, 2021
The Ritual

I thought this movie did everything extremely well, from the scares, to the character development, and for having one of the coolest monster designs I've seen in recent years. Could these guys solve their problems by just talking about their emotions? Yes. But then we wouldn't have this sweet monster at the end of it.

  • Director: David Bruckner
  • Release date: October 13, 2017
The Descent

This movie has awesome creatures in it. I'd say it's worth a watch just for the atmosphere and the monsters alone. The dialogue and characters are lacking but they really capture the feeling of claustrophobia. My one main gripe is that the US and UK versions have different endings with very different tones. I watched the one with the US ending and thought it was really silly, especially compared to the UK.

  • Director: Neil Marshall
  • Release date: August 4, 2006
