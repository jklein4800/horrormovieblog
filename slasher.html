Henry: Portrait of a Serial Killer

Based off the crimes of Henry Lee Lucas, this movie is intense, raw, and offers an unflinching look at crime and murder.

  • Director: John McNaughton
  • Release date: January 5, 1990
Silent Night, Deadly Night

This movie is goofy, very 80's, but is a repeat around the holidays. It features "Warm Side of the Door", by Morgan Aimes, which will really get stuck in your head unfortunately.

  • Director: Charles Sellier
  • Release date: November 9, 1984
Halloween (2018)

I only saw this once, when it came out in theaters, and aside from a few cool kills I remember feeling pretty disappointed and bored by this movie. Eventually I'd like to give it a rewatch just to see if I judged it too harshly.

  • Director: David Gordon Green
  • Release date: October 19, 2018
