Recent Watches

Annabelle: Creation

I threw this on as background noise while working and was extremely surprised that it was actually really fun. It's not without it's hokey moments but I thought it had great atmosphere and good scares.

  • Director: David F. Sandberg
  • Release date: August 11, 2017
Hunter Hunter

A story about survival. I thought this film was very effective emotionally. Very good pacing and powerful ending.

  • Director: Shawn Linden
  • Release date: December 18, 2020
The Conjuring 1 & 2

After hearing so much hype about these movies, I finally got to watching them. I prefer the second to the first but was quite bored with both of them. I didn't like the portrayal of Ed and Lorraine Warren, and most of the scares were very predictable. I did really like "The Crooked Man" scenes but that was about it.

  • Director: James Wan
  • Release date: July 19, 2013, May 13, 2016
