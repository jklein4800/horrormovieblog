Host

I was pleasantly surprised by this one. Host came out at the end of 2020 and really captured a lot of the COVID-19 things we were all experiencing. It packed a punch with well-timed jump scares. It had great pacing for such a short run-time(56 minutes) and I love that it was made using Zoom and using practical effects.

  • Director: Rob Savage
  • Release date: December 4, 2020
Terrified (Aterrados)

Genuinely frightening and chock-full of disturbing imagery. Absolutely worth a watch.

  • Director: Demián Rugna
  • Release date: May 3, 2018
The Autopsy of Jane Doe

The last act got a little goofy but I thought this movie was perfectly spooky and had a great atmosphere.

  • Director: André Øvredal
  • Release date: December 21, 2016
